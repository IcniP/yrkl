WHY CORAL REEFS MATTER

Safeguarding coastal communities

Coral reefs act as natural barriers, protecting coastal areas from waves, erosion, and extreme weather.

More biodiversity than rainforests

Despite covering less than 1% of the ocean floor, coral reefs support an extraordinary diversity of marine life.

Healthy reefs, healthy people

Strong reef ecosystems sustain fisheries, tourism, and food security for millions worldwide.

Long-term economic value

Healthy reefs create sustainable livelihoods while preserving marine balance for the future.
